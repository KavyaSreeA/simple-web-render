About

A Journey Rooted in
Curiosity & Compassion

Big "O" Minds was born from a simple yet profound belief: that every person carries within them an extraordinary potential, waiting to be awakened through awareness.

As an ICF-certified coach, MBTI & FIRO practitioner, and Learning & Development partner, I've had the privilege of walking alongside hundreds of individuals and teams — helping them move from confusion to clarity, from stagnation to meaningful momentum.

The "O" in Big O Minds isn't just a letter — it's a Circle of Awareness. A reminder that growth is not linear; it's expansive, circular, and deeply personal. There is no judgment here. Only curious exploration and genuine partnership.
